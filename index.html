About

At three years old, her fingers first touched piano keys.

Raised in South Florida with Colombian roots, she spent her childhood at the piano for hours, singing until the sun went down. Her sound is pop R&B with a Latin twist — classic yet entirely new.

Drawing from the timeless grace of Nina Simone, the sensual cool of Sade, and the crossover magic of Selena, Béka Maria's style emerges as something entirely her own: raw, magnetic, and fearless.

A National YoungArts Winner and Sony/ATV All Access alum, she founded her own company while still in college, earned her degree in Songwriting from Belmont University, and claimed the 2024 Hip Hop R&B Showcase title.

"Music for the ones who feel too much, want too much, and are done apologizing for it."
