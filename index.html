The Artist

Meet
Béka Maria

"She wasn't just practicing. She was becoming."

At three years old, her fingers first touched piano keys. Raised in South Florida with Colombian roots, she spent her childhood at the piano for hours, singing until the sun went down.

With a divine feminine energy and a deeply emotional delivery, Béka Maria's performances transcend boundaries, leaving a lasting impression on all who listen. Her sound is pop R&B with a Latin twist… classic yet entirely new.

Drawing from the timeless grace of Nina Simone, the sensual cool of Sade, and the crossover magic of Selena, Béka Maria's style emerges as something entirely her own: raw, magnetic, and fearless.

A National YoungArts Winner and Sony/ATV All Access alum, Béka Maria is not waiting for permission to build her dream. She founded Bekah Jayne Inc. while still in college, earned her degree in Songwriting from Belmont University, and claimed the 2024 Hip Hop R&B Showcase title.

She's not here to fit the mold.
She's here to eat the world.
